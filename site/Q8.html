8. Share a food recipe.

 1. Toast bread. 2. While toasting, open the avocado and pull it out. 3. Put the avocado in the bowl and mash it with the fork. (works better than using a spoon) 4. Put right amount of salt and pepper. 5. When your toast is done, put the mashed avocado on the toast. 6. Have a nice avocado toast. It's just that simple and delicious.
