Question 9

9. In terms of website design, which one is more important？ Aesthetics or practicality?

  I personally prefer aesthetics over practicality, but I think it depends on the intention. When it comes to a personal artistic website or web using for performance art, aesthetics must be considered first. However, when the web is aiming at something public or something practica, surely practicality must come up first.
